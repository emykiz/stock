Communications Specialist

Contract: Part Time
Closing Date: Until filled
Location: Remote

The Communications Specialist is responsible for directing and managing Stocknet Institute’s digital content, campaigns and marketing on the company’s social platforms, as well as managing public relations and corporate communications. We are looking for an analytical, creative and versatile person with a passion for delivering exceptional creative campaigns. This role entails freedom to work on one’s own terms with no physical attendance or set hourly requirements.

Responsibilities:

  • Using initiative, creativity and industry knowledge to generate engaging content.
  • Engaging new and existing clients, increasing social exposure and generating new business.
  • Developing and executing a digital marketing strategy in line with the company’s objectives. 
  • Creating and managing PR campaigns
  • Outreaching with creators and industry figures.

Requirements: 

  • Significant experience in running a successful social channel.
  • Thorough understanding of the social platforms’ algorithms.
  • Native level written and verbal English language.
  • Experience in creating appealing digital content and campaigns.
  • Comfortable camera presence and great oral communication skills.
  • Creative thinker by nature with a thirst for managing a brand.
  • An understanding of trading, foreign exchange markets and trading technology.
